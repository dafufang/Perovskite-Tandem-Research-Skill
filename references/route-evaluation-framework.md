## Weighted criteria
| Criterion | Default weight | What to check |
|---|---:|---|
| Efficiency and energy-yield potential | 15% | certified PCE, outdoor yield, bifaciality, temperature/spectral sensitivity |
| Stability and reliability path | 20% | MPP, damp heat, thermal cycling, encapsulation, degradation rate |
| Process compatibility | 15% | substrate, temperature, atmosphere, equipment, bottom-cell compatibility |
| Scale-up difficulty | 15% | area, uniformity, yield, scribing, throughput |
| Cost potential | 10% | capex, material utilization, process time, yield, lifetime |
| IP and competition | 10% | crowded patents, dominant players, freedom-to-operate risk |
| Testing/certification path | 5% | availability of certification route, module standards, reliability testing |
| Target-application fit | 10% | module, flexible, BIPV, high-efficiency, lightweight, specialty PV |

## Recommendation labels
- Go: strong evidence, clear differentiation, manageable risk.
- Conditional Go: promising but requires targeted validation before full investment.
- Watch: strategically relevant but not ready for active development.
- No-Go: poor fit, weak evidence, unfavorable economics, or excessive risk.
