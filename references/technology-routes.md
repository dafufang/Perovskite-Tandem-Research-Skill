# Technology Routes

## Perovskite/silicon 2T monolithic tandem
- Goal: exceed single-junction silicon efficiency with a monolithic two-terminal stack.
- Typical stack: silicon bottom cell, recombination/interconnection layer, wide-bandgap perovskite top cell, transparent electrode, grid/encapsulation.
- Strengths: high efficiency potential, compatible with high-power silicon module strategy.
- Bottlenecks: current matching, textured silicon coverage, TCO damage, interconnect loss, top-cell stability, module reliability.
- Best fit: high-efficiency modules where silicon platform access exists.
- Avoid/Watch: if no silicon bottom-cell access, no low-temperature process capability, or no stability/encapsulation testing.

## Perovskite/silicon 4T tandem
- Goal: combine semitransparent perovskite top cell and silicon bottom cell without current matching.
- Strengths: easier subcell optimization, faster proof-of-concept, useful for semitransparent applications.
- Bottlenecks: optical losses, transparent electrodes, system complexity, cost.
- Best fit: rapid screening, BIPV, semitransparent concepts, early-stage validation.

## All-perovskite tandem
- Goal: lightweight, low-temperature, potentially low-cost tandem using wide-bandgap top cell and Sn-Pb narrow-bandgap bottom cell.
- Strengths: high power-to-weight, flexible potential, all-thin-film manufacturing.
- Bottlenecks: Sn oxidation, Voc deficit, stability, interconnect layers, scale-up.
- Best fit: flexible, lightweight, BIPV, vehicle-integrated, specialty PV.

## Perovskite/CIGS tandem
- Goal: combine semitransparent perovskite with low-bandgap thin-film CIGS.
- Strengths: flexible/thin-film compatibility.
- Bottlenecks: CIGS availability/quality, optical loss, integration and smaller ecosystem.
- Best fit: flexible or thin-film applications.

## Wide-bandgap perovskite top cells
- Goal: provide high-voltage, high-transparency top absorber for tandems.
- Bottlenecks: halide segregation, Voc deficit, interfacial recombination, operational stability.
- Critical tests: light-soaking PL/EL, MPP stability, bandgap shift, tandem integration.

## Sn-Pb low-bandgap bottom cells
- Goal: absorb near-infrared photons in all-perovskite tandems.
- Bottlenecks: Sn oxidation, self-doping, low Voc, air sensitivity.
- Critical tests: oxygen sensitivity, Voc loss, IR EQE, shelf and operational stability.

## Slot-die / blade coating
- Goal: scalable solution deposition.
- Strengths: manufacturable path, lower capex potential.
- Bottlenecks: film uniformity, drying/crystallization, process window, atmosphere control.

## Vapor deposition / co-evaporation
- Goal: controlled, conformal, scalable thin-film deposition.
- Strengths: textured compatibility, thickness control, solvent-free integration.
- Bottlenecks: capex, throughput, material utilization, composition control.

## Module integration
- Goal: convert cells to minimodules/modules.
- Bottlenecks: scribing loss, interconnection, encapsulation, geometric fill factor, edge ingress, uniformity, yield.
