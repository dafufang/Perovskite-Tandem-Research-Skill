# R&D Decision Framework

Use this when the user asks whether to start, continue, prioritize, fund, or stop a direction.

## Required output

# 研发决策建议

## 决策结论
Choose one: Go / Conditional Go / Watch / No-Go.

## 关键假设
List assumptions about target product, equipment, timeline, application, and risk tolerance.

## 判断依据

## Key risks
Separate scientific, engineering, scale-up, reliability, IP, cost, and market risks.

## 3-month validation plan
Give a small set of experiments that can disprove or validate the most important assumptions.

## 6-month milestones
Define measurable milestones: efficiency, area, stability, reproducibility, yield, process window.

## Go/No-Go criteria
Use numeric criteria when possible. If not enough information exists, define evidence thresholds.

## Decision notes
Do not equate high-impact papers with immediate commercialization. Do not treat company announcements as verified technical maturity without supporting evidence.
