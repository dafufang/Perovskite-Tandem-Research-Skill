# Source Priority

## P0 - Highest priority
Use these for records, certification, and technical claims whenever available.
- NREL Best Research-Cell Efficiency Chart
- NREL official releases
- Fraunhofer ISE / CalLab
- JEST, AIST, Newport, or other recognized certification labs
- Nature, Science, Nature Energy, Joule
- Energy & Environmental Science
- Advanced Energy Materials
- ACS Energy Letters
- Progress in Photovoltaics
- IEEE Journal of Photovoltaics
- Solar RRL

## P1 - Strong supporting sources
- Company official releases, clearly labeled as company claims unless independently verified
- University and national-lab releases
- IEA PVPS
- Fraunhofer ISE Photovoltaics Report
- PV Magazine, PV Tech, TaiyangNews, SolarPower Europe
- Conference proceedings when technical details are sufficient

## P2 - Leads only
- Wikipedia
- Blogs
- Social media
- General news without technical detail
- Marketing material
- Preprints, unless labeled clearly and checked against final publication status

## Credibility rules
- Prefer primary sources over secondary reports.
- Prefer certification bodies over company press releases for efficiency records.
- Prefer peer-reviewed data over marketing claims.
- Use secondary media only to locate primary sources or summarize industry context.
