# Manufacturing and Scale-Up Checks

## Process routes

### Solution coating
Includes spin coating, blade coating, slot-die coating, spray, inkjet, and roll-to-roll variants.
Check: ink stability, wetting, drying, crystallization, atmosphere, defect density, process window, coating speed, area uniformity.

### Vapor deposition
Includes evaporation, co-evaporation, vapor-assisted conversion, and hybrid processes.
Check: conformality, composition control, substrate temperature, throughput, material utilization, capex, maintenance.

### Hybrid routes
Combine solution and vapor steps. Check whether the hybrid process adds complexity that is justified by performance or reliability.

## Module integration
Check:
- aperture/module area
- geometric fill factor
- P1/P2/P3 scribing loss
- interconnect resistance
- edge isolation
- encapsulant compatibility
- barrier properties
- moisture ingress
- thermal expansion mismatch
- yield and reworkability

## Silicon tandem integration
Check:
- SHJ, TOPCon, IBC, or other bottom-cell platform
- low-temperature budget
- textured surface compatibility
- sputtering damage
- optical reflection/transmission
- current matching under varied spectra

## Manufacturing decision questions
- Does the result depend on spin coating or small-area tricks?
- Is the substrate industrially realistic?
- Are process tolerances quantified?
- Is yield reported or only champion-cell performance?
- Is encapsulation compatible with the stack?
- Are reliability tests performed after module integration?
