# Paper Review Template

Use this structure for uploaded papers, named papers, or DOI/title questions.

# 论文精读

## 一句话结论
State the real contribution and whether it is mainly scientific, engineering, or commercialization-relevant.

## 研究对象与问题定位
- 技术路线：single-junction / perovskite-silicon 2T / 4T / all-perovskite / perovskite-CIGS / module
- 论文要解决的问题
- 与已有工作的关系

## 器件结构
- substrate / bottom cell
- top-cell absorber and bandgap
- HTL / ETL / SAM / passivation
- recombination or interconnection layer
- transparent conductive electrode
- metal electrode / grid
- encapsulation if any

## 关键创新
Classify novelty:
1. material/composition
2. interface/contact
3. device architecture
4. optical management
5. processing/manufacturing
6. characterization/diagnostics
7. stability/encapsulation

## 稳定性分析
- encapsulated or unencapsulated
- MPP tracking or storage/open circuit
- light intensity
- temperature
- humidity
- duration
- T80/T90/remaining PCE
- sample count
- outdoor or indoor
- evidence level: strong / medium / weak / only a lead

## 机制证据
List the measurements supporting the claimed mechanism, such as PL/TRPL, EL, EQE, XRD, GIWAXS, XPS, ToF-SIMS, Kelvin probe, DLTS, impedance, temperature-dependent JV, or modeling.

## 可复现性判断
- materials availability
- process complexity
- process-window sensitivity
- equipment dependence
- environmental sensitivity
- whether controls are sufficient

## 放大与产业化相关性
- cell/minimodule/module area
- compatible substrate or bottom cell
- scalable process evidence
- encapsulation and reliability evidence
- equipment/cost/yield implications

## 对研发决策的启发
- worth adopting
- worth testing with modifications
- watch only
- not recommended
