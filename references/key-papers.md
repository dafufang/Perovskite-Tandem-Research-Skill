# Representative Literature Cards

Use these cards as a static map of important literature and themes. Verify exact titles, DOI, efficiency values, certified status, and latest follow-up work before citing them as facts.

## A. Foundations and single-junction perovskites

### A001 - Meso-superstructured organometal halide perovskite solar cells
- Direction: early single-junction perovskite PV
- Theme: transition from dye-sensitized concepts to solid-state perovskite photovoltaics
- Typical use: explain why perovskite PV became a major research field
- R&D insight: background only; not a direct guide for current tandem integration

### A002 - Mixed-cation mixed-halide absorbers for tandem top cells
- Direction: wide-bandgap perovskites
- Theme: compositional engineering for bandgap tuning and stability
- Key checks: halide segregation, Voc deficit, bandgap, light stability
- R&D insight: wide-bandgap stability is a core tandem bottleneck

### A003 - High-efficiency single-junction perovskite loss analysis
- Direction: single-junction efficiency limits
- Theme: optical/electrical loss breakdown and practical efficiency ceiling
- Key checks: non-radiative recombination, series resistance, current losses
- R&D insight: use as a diagnostic framework, not as proof of tandem manufacturability

## B. Perovskite/silicon 2T tandems

### B001 - Early monolithic perovskite/silicon tandem with improved stability
- Direction: 2T perovskite/silicon
- Theme: monolithic integration, transparent electrodes, interface protection
- Key checks: p-i-n top cell, buffer layers, TCO damage, stabilized output
- R&D insight: integration layers are as important as absorber quality

### B002 - Fully textured monolithic perovskite/silicon tandem
- Direction: 2T perovskite/silicon
- Theme: compatibility with textured silicon and optical management
- Key checks: conformal coverage, reflection reduction, current density, certified area
- R&D insight: textured compatibility is a major industrial-relevance gate

### B003 - Solution-processed perovskite on textured crystalline silicon
- Direction: 2T perovskite/silicon
- Theme: scalable solution processing on textured substrates
- Key checks: wetting, crystallization control, top-cell uniformity, subcell current matching
- R&D insight: useful for evaluating whether coating routes can compete with vapor routes

### B004 - Slot-die coated perovskite/silicon tandem demonstrations
- Direction: scalable 2T tandem process
- Theme: slot-die coating, drying, and film uniformity
- Key checks: coating speed, process window, area, yield, reproducibility
- R&D insight: bridges lab spin coating and pilot-line process thinking

### B005 - Outdoor performance and stability of perovskite/silicon tandems
- Direction: field performance
- Theme: spectral variation, current matching, temperature, energy yield
- Key checks: outdoor data, modeled vs measured yield, climate dependence
- R&D insight: STC efficiency does not guarantee outdoor energy-yield advantage

### B006 - Perovskite/silicon tandem review and outlook papers
- Direction: review
- Theme: materials, interfaces, textured silicon, stability, module scale-up
- Key checks: publication date, record table currency, scope of review
- R&D insight: use recent reviews to orient route selection, then verify current data

### B007 - Detailed-balance and efficiency-limit analyses for perovskite/silicon tandems
- Direction: theoretical limit
- Theme: bandgap selection, current matching, optical losses
- Key checks: assumptions on radiative limit, parasitic absorption, temperature, spectrum
- R&D insight: useful for upper-bound logic; not sufficient for project selection

### B008 - Realistic operating-condition analyses for tandems
- Direction: energy-yield modeling
- Theme: outdoor spectra, temperature, current mismatch, bifaciality
- Key checks: climate data, module assumptions, albedo, degradation
- R&D insight: route decisions should consider yield, not only nameplate PCE

### B009 - Fill-factor loss analysis in monolithic tandems
- Direction: device diagnostics
- Theme: series resistance, shunts, recombination, silicon bottom-cell behavior
- Key checks: light-intensity JV, EL, Suns-Voc, subcell-limited FF
- R&D insight: build FF-loss diagnostics early in tandem programs

## C. Perovskite/silicon 4T and semitransparent devices

### C001 - Semitransparent perovskite cells for 4T tandem applications
- Direction: 4T tandem
- Theme: transparent electrodes and NIR transmittance
- Key checks: average visible/NIR transmittance, top-cell efficiency, bottom-cell current retention
- R&D insight: strong screening platform for top-cell materials

### C002 - High-transparency 4T perovskite/silicon tandems
- Direction: 4T tandem
- Theme: optical coupling and electrode optimization
- Key checks: total efficiency, optical stack, parasitic absorption
- R&D insight: useful where system complexity is acceptable or semitransparency is valued

## D. All-perovskite tandems

### D001 - Bandgap-optimized perovskite-perovskite tandem photovoltaics
- Direction: all-perovskite tandem
- Theme: top/bottom bandgap pairing
- Key checks: wide-bandgap stability, narrow-bandgap voltage deficit, current matching
- R&D insight: establishes the core design logic for all-perovskite tandems

### D002 - Low-bandgap mixed Sn-Pb perovskites with long carrier lifetimes
- Direction: narrow-bandgap bottom cell
- Theme: Sn-Pb absorber quality and IR response
- Key checks: Sn oxidation, p-type self-doping, Voc deficit, EQE
- R&D insight: bottom-cell quality is central to all-perovskite performance

### D003 - Certified all-perovskite tandem cells using antioxidant/passivation strategies
- Direction: all-perovskite tandem
- Theme: Sn-Pb stabilization and certified efficiency
- Key checks: area, certification, stability, bottom-cell chemistry
- R&D insight: antioxidant/passivation approaches are important but must be screened for process compatibility

### D004 - Surface-potential or interfacial engineering to maximize tandem voltage
- Direction: all-perovskite tandem
- Theme: interface energetics and recombination suppression
- Key checks: Voc, QFLS, subcell-resolved losses
- R&D insight: interface energy alignment and recombination diagnostics are key capabilities

### D005 - Flexible all-perovskite tandem demonstrations
- Direction: flexible tandem
- Theme: lightweight devices, low-temperature processing, mechanical stability
- Key checks: bending tests, encapsulation, operational stability
- R&D insight: strong for weight-sensitive applications, but reliability evidence must be strong

### D006 - Vapor-deposited wide-bandgap perovskite in all-perovskite tandems
- Direction: vapor/hybrid processing
- Theme: conformality, dual-interface optimization, scalable deposition
- Key checks: equipment complexity, film uniformity, interface quality
- R&D insight: relevant when vacuum infrastructure exists or textured/conformal coverage is needed

## E. Stability and failure mechanisms

### E001 - ISOS-style stability protocols
- Direction: reliability testing
- Theme: standardized stress conditions and reporting
- Key checks: light, temperature, humidity, MPP, encapsulation, duration
- R&D insight: convert all stability claims into comparable evidence levels

### E002 - Ion migration and hysteresis studies
- Direction: device physics
- Theme: ion migration, interfacial accumulation, hysteresis, degradation
- Key checks: scan-rate dependence, pre-bias, transient response, MPP tracking
- R&D insight: JV alone is insufficient for trustworthy device assessment

### E003 - Halide segregation in wide-bandgap perovskites
- Direction: wide-bandgap stability
- Theme: photoinduced phase segregation and Br-rich absorber instability
- Key checks: PL shift, EL, light soaking, recovery, MPP stability
- R&D insight: top-cell stability is often the limiting factor in silicon tandems

### E004 - Lead leakage, encapsulation, and environmental safety
- Direction: environmental reliability
- Theme: lead containment, module breakage, recycling, encapsulants
- Key checks: sealed modules, water ingress, lead sequestration, end-of-life plan
- R&D insight: commercialization evaluation must include environmental and safety controls

## F. Manufacturing, modules, TEA, and LCA

### F001 - Slot-die/blade coating for perovskite cells and modules
- Direction: scalable coating
- Theme: ink engineering, drying, crystallization, uniformity
- Key checks: area, speed, yield, environmental tolerance
- R&D insight: process-window width matters more than champion-cell efficiency

### F002 - Vapor deposition and co-evaporation for tandem integration
- Direction: scalable vapor processing
- Theme: conformality, layer control, textured silicon compatibility
- Key checks: capex, throughput, material utilization, process control
- R&D insight: strong integration potential but higher equipment barrier

### F003 - Techno-economic analysis of perovskite/silicon tandem modules
- Direction: commercialization
- Theme: cost, yield, lifetime, efficiency premium, manufacturing cost
- Key checks: assumptions on lifetime, degradation, capex, yield, throughput
- R&D insight: high efficiency only creates value if lifetime and yield support economics

### F004 - Life-cycle analysis of perovskite tandem photovoltaics
- Direction: sustainability
- Theme: energy payback, carbon footprint, material toxicity, recycling
- Key checks: lifetime assumptions, module efficiency, encapsulation, end-of-life handling
- R&D insight: useful for product strategy, financing narrative, and sustainability claims
