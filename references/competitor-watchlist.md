# Competitor and Institution Watchlist

Use this list to guide dynamic searches. Current status must always be verified.

## Oxford PV
- Focus: perovskite-on-silicon tandem commercialization.
- Track: certified cell/module efficiency, product claims, production status, reliability claims, partnerships.
- Rule: distinguish company announcements from third-party certifications.

## LONGi
- Focus: high-efficiency perovskite/silicon tandem records and silicon platform integration.
- Track: certified efficiency, device area, bottom-cell platform, module translation.

## Helmholtz-Zentrum Berlin (HZB)
- Focus: high-efficiency perovskite/silicon tandems, textured silicon, interfaces, optical management.
- Track: peer-reviewed papers, certified cells, transfer to scalable processes.

## KAUST
- Focus: tandem architectures, outdoor testing, interfaces, stability, all-perovskite and silicon tandems.
- Track: outdoor energy yield studies, high-efficiency demonstrations, stability methods.

## NREL
- Focus: efficiency chart, reliability, materials, certification context, PV analysis.
- Track: record chart updates and technical reports.

## Fraunhofer ISE
- Focus: certification, PV reports, tandem research, industrial relevance.
- Track: CalLab results, photovoltaic reports, technology assessments.

## EPFL / University of Oxford / University of Toronto / UNSW and other universities
- Focus: leading academic advances in perovskite materials, tandems, and device physics.
- Track: papers, patents, spinouts, collaboration signals.

## Microquanta, UtmoLight, GCL Perovskite, Saule Technologies, Swift Solar, Tandem PV
- Focus: modules, pilots, commercial claims, flexible/BIPV products, funding, partnerships.
- Track: certified module efficiency, area, reliability, shipments, production capacity, product warranties.
- Rule: company-reported scale-up claims require independent corroboration where possible.
